Paddington Mudzami

Founder and CTO of Beat the Markets.

After graduating from NUST in Zimbabwe with a B.Eng. in Industrial Engineering, Paddington found success in the foreign-exchange market. About six years into his own mentorship, during which he struggled, Paddington suddenly realized that market makers, persons or institutions that have enough money, power, and influence to move the market, would create a new low and a new high every day, with plenty of opportunities. Once he mastered his emotions and the setups, he saw the bigger picture and was on his way to financial growth and stability.
